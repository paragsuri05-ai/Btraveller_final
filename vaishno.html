Vaishnov Devi Yatra by Helicopter Same Day Tour

Mata Vaishno Devi Yatra in the Trikuta hills of Jammu and Kashmir is one of the most revered pilgrimages in India. A spiritual journey that reverberates with cries of ‘Jai Mata Di’, the holy cave of Mata Vaishno Devi is enshrined with legends and stories that add to the mystical charms of the place. A manifestation of coming together of Maha Kali, Maha Lakshmi and Maha Saraswati, Mata Vaishno Devi is said to have slain Bhairon nath who pursued her for amorous reasons at the very mouth of this cave.

Nothing can be more fulfilling for the devout than getting a glimpse of Matarani despite paucity of time. We understands the constraints of the pilgrims and has a comfortable itinerary chalked out to fulfill their necessities. Same day Vaishno Devi tour includes return airfare from Delhi, airport transfer from Jammu and back , helicopter ride to Saanjhi Chat and back and VIP darshan with dedicated personnel for a smooth transition.

Co-ordinator at Jammu airport and conduct them to the helicopter. He/she shall also receive them on their way back from Sanchi Chaat and guide them further to the hotel/ flight. VIP Dharshan.

GUIDELINES FOR PASSENGERS

There is very limited space for luggage in the chopper. Only 5 kg luggage per passenger will be permitted.
Passengers bringing excess baggage would not be allowed to take the same in the chopper.
Passengers should carry only handbags as suitcases cannot be kept in the helicopter.
All temples in Chardham are located in high altitude areas. Passengers are advice to carry regular medical kit.
At these locations, it becomes cold throughout, so passengers must carry woolen clothes with them.
Children above the age of 2 years will be considered full ticket
There is no provision of concession to children or senior citizens
All properties lodge with hot water, towels, shower and western toilets.

  • Day 01

    DAY 1 Jammu Airport - Sanjii Chaat Helipad - Jammu Airport (Same Day)

    9:30 – 10:00AM : Arrive at Jammu Airport and get a transfer to the Helicopter from Jammu airport which will directly take you to Shanji Chaat.
    10:15 – 10:45AM : Arrive at Sanjii Chaat Helipad. You can proceed to the Shrine for VIP darshan. Shrine is 2.5KM from Sanji Chaat, Palki/ Mule is also available from Helipad to Shrine and back.
    1:00 – 1:30PM : Fly from Sanji Chaat to Jammu Airport and lunch at Jammu Airport.
