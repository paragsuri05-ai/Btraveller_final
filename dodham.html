GUIDELINES FOR PASSENGERS

There is very limited space for luggage in the chopper. Only 5 kg luggage per passenger will be permitted.
Passengers bringing excess baggage would not be allowed to take the same in the chopper.
Passengers should carry only handbags as suitcases cannot be kept in the helicopter.
All temples in Chardham are located in high altitude areas. Passengers are advice to carry regular medical kit.
At these locations, it becomes cold throughout, so passengers must carry woolen clothes with them.
Children above the age of 2 years will be considered full ticket
There is no provision of concession to children or senior citizens
All properties lodge with hot water, towels, shower and western toilets.

Recommended Itinerary Do Dham Yatra Same Day Tour

  • Day 01

    DAY 1 Kedarnath & Badrinath Dham Charter Itinerary
    KEDARNATH
    1145 AM Departure sahastradhara helipad /Dehradun
    1230 P M Arrival Kedarnath helipad
    1430 PM Departure Kedar to Sirsi (After Darshan at Kedar)

    After arrival, passenger will directly go for Darshan. Temple is just 500 M away from helipad and takes 15 minutes to reach. Kedarnath temple , at an height of 3,583 m , on the shores of Mandakini river , a tributary of Ganga, is an impressive stone edifice of unknown date. Among the twelve JyotirLingas of Bhagwan Shankara , the one at Kedarnath is located in the snow - covered area of the Himalayas . This JyotirLinga can be visited only during six months in a year


    Badrinath
    1430 P M Departure Sirsi to Badrinath
    1500 P M Arrival Badrinath Helipad
    1645 PM Departure Badri to Sahastradhara (After Darshan at Badri)

    After arrival , passengers will go to Hotel Sarovar Portico for Lunch which is close to temple. T he Badrinath Temple also known as the Badrinarayan Temple, located in Uttarakhand's Badrinath town, is considered as the most holy among the char dhams. It is among the 108 Divya Desams dedicated to Lord Vishnu, who is worshipped in this temple as Shri Badrinath
